# VFXB Timeline - Complete Feature Guide

## ✅ IMPLEMENTED FEATURES

### 🎯 How to Access
1. Upload a video on the initial screen
2. Click the **"Studio"** button in the **CENTER TOP** of the screen
3. The timeline appears at the **BOTTOM** of the Studio view

---

## 🖱️ DRAG & DROP FUNCTIONALITY

### **Drag Clips**
- **Click and hold** any clip
- **Drag left/right** to move it on the timeline
- **Release** to drop
- ✅ Toast notification: "Clip moved"

### **Resize Clips**
- **Hover over clip edges** (left or right side)
- **Resize handles appear** (white overlay on edges)
- **Click and drag** the edge to resize
- ✅ Toast notification: "Clip resized"

### **Select Clips**
- **Click** any clip to select it
- **Blue ring** appears around selected clip
- **Action menu appears** above the clip with 3 buttons

---

## 🤖 AI FEATURES

### **Access AI Features Panel**
**Method 1: Track-Level AI**
1. **Hover over track name** (Video 1, Audio 1, Text 1)
2. **Blue sparkle icon ✨ appears**
3. **Click sparkle icon**
4. **AI Features modal opens** with 6 AI tools

**Method 2: Clip Actions**
1. **Click any clip** to select it
2. Use the **action menu** (Split/Copy/Delete)

## ✂️ CLIP ACTIONS MENU

When you **click a clip**, a menu appears above it with:

1. **✂️ Split** - Divide clip in half at current position
2. **📋 Duplicate** - Copy clip to timeline
3. **🗑️ Delete** - Remove clip from timeline

---

## 🎛️ TIMELINE CONTROLS

### **Toolbar (Top of Timeline)**
- **▶️ Play/Pause** - Control playback
- **✂️ Cut** - Cut tool
- **📚 Layer** - Layer management
- **📋 Copy** - Copy tool
- **⏱️ Timecode Display** - Shows current time / total duration
- **🔍 Zoom Controls** - ➖ Zoom Out / ➕ Zoom In (25% to 400%)

### **Track Controls (Left Sidebar)**
- **👁️ Eye Icon** - Toggle track visibility
- **🔊 Volume Icon** - Mute/unmute track
- **🔒 Lock Icon** - Lock/unlock track (prevents editing)
